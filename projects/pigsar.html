PIGSAR (Package for Integrative Gene Set Analysis in R)

The most common method of performing Gene Set Analysis (GSA) for miRNAs is to find the miRNA targets and then performing GSA for such targets. For GSA of lncRNAs, it is common to study correlations between lncRNAs and mRNAs. Both scenarios can lead to complex networks of either miRNA-target or lncRNA-mRNA interactions.

Also, current databases of ncRNA interactions depict a landscape of multiple biological entities (mRNAs, miRNAs, lncRNAs, circRNAs, sncRNAs, RNA-binding proteins, and others) interacting with each other in complex networks, which makes us think that the correlation structure may be more complex than simple pairwise correlations and, therefore, network-based methods applied to integrative correlation networks of RNAs and RBPs becomes an interesting area of research.

PIGSAR is a project that aims to build an R package to perform such an integrative GSA of coding and non-coding RNA data, based on other existing R and Bioconductor packages.
